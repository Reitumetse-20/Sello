About Presley

Presley Oageng Chweneyagae (1984–2025) was a South African actor best known for his role in the Oscar-winning film Tsotsi. Born in Mafikeng, he began acting in local theatre before achieving global acclaim.

Achievements

  • Lead role in Tsotsi (2005) – Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film
  • SAFTA Award Winner for Best Actor – The River
  • Co-writer of the stage play Relativity
  • Starred in Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom
